# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, accessible user interfaces and experiences. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to translate product vision into usable designs and ensure features meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and user experience flows
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with Product Managers to understand requirements
- Partner with Developers to ensure design feasibility
- Create design specifications, wireframes, and prototypes
- Advocate for user accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible products that users love
- Reduce support burden through excellent UX
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- Participation in planning to define design requirements
- Retrospectives to incorporate user feedback
- Collaboration with Developers on implementation details

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain deployment pipelines, infrastructure, and monitoring systems. They enable reliable, automated, and observable releases while ensuring system performance and security.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain deployment pipelines and CI/CD infrastructure
- Manage cloud infrastructure and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes, etc.)
- Implement automated testing, monitoring, and alerting
- Troubleshoot production issues and optimize performance
- Document runbooks and disaster recovery procedures
- Collaborate with Developers and QA to automate quality gates

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable deployments with minimal risk
- Maintain high system uptime and performance
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Automate repetitive operational tasks

### Typical Communication
- Deployment coordination during release windows
- Infrastructure and performance reviews
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Technical documentation and runbooks

---

## Data Analysts

### Role Summary
Data Analysts interpret data, track key metrics, and provide insights that inform product and business decisions. They bridge data and strategy to ensure initiatives are validated by evidence.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics aligned with project goals
- Analyze user behavior, feature adoption, and performance data
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Identify trends, anomalies, and optimization opportunities
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Provide data-informed recommendations to Product and Project Managers

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based decision-making
- Provide visibility into project outcomes and ROI
- Identify opportunities for product and process optimization
- Support rapid iteration through data insights

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews with PM and Project Manager
- Dashboard and reporting updates
- Data analysis during retrospectives
- Ad-hoc analysis to support critical decisions

---

## Customer Support & Success

### Role Summary
Customer Support and Success professionals represent the voice of the customer. They surface user feedback, support issues, and product insights that inform prioritization and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Manage customer inquiries and support tickets
- Identify common pain points and feature requests
- Escalate critical issues and bugs discovered in production
- Gather customer feedback and share insights with the product team
- Participate in product planning to advocate for customer needs
- Create and maintain help documentation and guides

### Goals
- Deliver exceptional customer experiences
- Reduce customer churn and increase satisfaction
- Bring customer insights into product prioritization
- Enable customers to succeed with the product

### Typical Communication
- Weekly insights sharing with Product and Project teams
- Participation in planning sessions to advocate for user needs
- Contribution to retrospectives with customer feedback
- Product feedback loops and feature request prioritization

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- Recognize that real projects may have overlapping responsibilities, and team members may wear multiple hats based on organization size and structure.
